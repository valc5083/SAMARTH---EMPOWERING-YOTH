FAQS​

How can I apply for the post of College Representative?

Click on the button to apply now!! Once the registration is complete, you will be notified through email id provided.

Apply now

What are my responsibilities as a CR?​

As a College Representative, you will be required to share Samarth's posts on all social media platforms, bring registration, organise events of Samarth in your college.

How to monitor my progress?

Check the document shared with you for your point, rank and progress.

How much difficult is it to achieve points?

Earning points are very easy and quick.

How difficult are the tasks?

The tasks are designed to improve your soft skills, marketing skills, publicity skills, social media handling.

How much time it takes to do a single task?

A single task doesn't take much time & the time depends on the weightage of the task.

How much point does each task carry?

Points carried by each task varies. For example, a post sharing task would carry 10-30 points and any event registration would bring 50-75 points. Note: These points are tentative and can change.

Can there be multiple College Ambassadors from a single college?

Yes, depending on the size of the college, Samarth can appoint multiple College Representatives.

How will my progress be monitored?

You will upload proofs of your work in the form; we will verify them and reward you with points that measure your progress. Please feel free to reach out to your mentors if you have any queries.
